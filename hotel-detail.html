C arved directly into the Alpine rock face, the Swiss Alps Retreat stands as a monument to raw material and absolute silence. Designed by Pritzker-winning architect Peter Zumthor, the property forces a dialogue between the harsh exterior environment and the meticulously curated warmth of its interior spaces.

The Concept

The design eliminates all unnecessary noise. There are no televisions, no clocks, and no distracting artworks. Instead, the focus is entirely on the shifting textures of quartzite, water, and mountain light as it enters through strategic geometric fissures.

The Materials

  • Structure Valser Quartzite
  • Furnishings Smoked Oak & Linen
  • Fixtures Brushed Brass
